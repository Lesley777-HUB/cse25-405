Common Questions

Quick answers before you reach out

Do you ship to other countries? +

Yes! We ship internationally. Shipping times and costs vary by destination. Orders within Botswana typically arrive within 2–4 business days.

What is your return policy? +

We offer 30-day returns on all full-price items in original condition. Sale items are final sale. Contact us to start a return.

How do I know what size to order? +

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a comfortable western fit. You can also contact us for personalized advice.

Are your leather products genuine? +

Absolutely. All leather products are crafted from genuine leather. We source responsibly and prioritize durability and quality above all.
